📖 How to Play

🎮 Controls

N/S/E/W or Arrows - Move
Look - Examine area
Take [item] - Pick up
Attack - Fight
Cast [spell] - Magic
Use [item] - Use item
I - Inventory
M - Map
Escape - Close panels

🗣️ Voice Commands (Alexa)

  • "North/South/East/West" - Move
  • "Look around" - Examine
  • "Take the [item]" - Pick up
  • "Attack" - Fight
  • "Cast fireball" - Use spell
  • "Use healing potion" - Heal
  • "Inventory" - View items
  • "Where am I" - Check location
